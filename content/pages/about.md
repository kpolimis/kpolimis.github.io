# Welcome

My name is Kivan and I am currently a Data Scientist with <a href="https://karna.com" target="_blank">Karna</a> in Houston, Texas. 

I also serve as a Chief Data Scientist with  <a href="https://www.analyticsbyatlas.com/" target="_blank">Atlas Analytics</a> and a Regional Affiliate at the University of Washington's <a href="https://csde.washington.edu" target="_blank">Center for the Study of Demography and Ecology (CSDE)</a>.

### Professional Philosophy
I operate at the intersection of Public Policy and Machine Learning Engineering My background in rigorous academic research (Demography/Causal Inference) allows me to ask the right questions, while my engineering experience allows me to build the scalable systems to answer them.

<hr>

### Featured Work & Open Source
* **Generative AI for Healthcare (Private):** Architected a privacy-first, multi-agent LLM orchestration framework using RAG to automate complex clinical documentation, reducing provider administrative load by 40% while maintaining strict PII compliance.
* **Real-Time Sports Analytics (Private):** Designed an end-to-end MLOps pipeline on AWS handling live data ingestion and daily retraining to model non-stationary performance metrics, successfully identifying market inefficiencies for the 2024-2025 NBA season.
* **Scikit-Learn Ecosystem:** Contributor to <a href="https://github.com/scikit-learn-contrib/forest-confidence-interval" target="_blank">forest-confidence-interval</a> and author of the associated <a href="https://joss.theoj.org/papers/10.21105/joss.00124" target="_blank">JOSS paper</a> for calculating variance in Random Forest predictions.
* **Large-Scale Optimization:** Former Technical Lead for <a href="https://github.com/DSSG-paratransit/main_repo" target="_blank">Data Science for Social Good</a>, where I architected paratransit routing systems that optimized thousands of daily trips.
* **Financial Transaction NLP:** Developed a transformer-based microservice to categorize noisy financial text, utilizing custom loss functions to solve extreme class imbalance in real-world banking data.

### Leadership & Mentorship
Engineering is a team sport. Beyond individual contribution, I focus on elevating the technical maturity of my teams.
* **Technical Mentorship:** I actively mentor junior data scientists, guiding them through the transition from experimental notebooks to production-grade software engineering practices.
* **Cross-Functional Communication:** Leveraged experience as a Policy Advisor to translate complex ML outcomes for non-technical stakeholders, ensuring alignment between engineering constraints and business goals.
